About Us

Burgerwars is a web application providing all your fast food service needs. With an advanced e-commerce system, we provide a quick and easy solution to online fast food ordering with just the push of a button.

Created in June of 2022, Burgerwars offers a diverse menu with interactive features that gives you the freedom to order what you want, the way you want it.
